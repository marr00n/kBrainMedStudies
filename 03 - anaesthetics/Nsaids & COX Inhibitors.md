## NSAIDs

- Anti-inflammatory action
- Doesn’t impair bone healing
- Concern in renal and GIT ulcers

## COX2 Inhibitors

- Doesn’t impair platelet function

## Contraindicated Populations
- Pregnancy
- Aspirin-sensitive asthma
- Gastric ulcerations
- Renal impaired (elderly, septic, CKD, critically ill, hypovolaemic, pre-eclampsia)
