- Primary inhibitory transmitter of the [[CNS]].
- Binds to [[GABA-A]] and [[GABA-B]] receptors.
- Triggers influx of chloride ions (or efflux of potassium), resulting in [[hyperpolarisation]] of the post-synaptic neuron.
- This makes the membrane potential more negative, preventing firing of action potentials.
