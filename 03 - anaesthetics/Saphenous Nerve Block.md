## Adductor Canal Block (ACB)

## Anatomy
- Sartorius muscle overlying everything
- Vastus medialis sits laterally
- Adductor longus is posteromedially

## Technique
- Frog leg the thigh
- Linear probe on anteromedial thigh – mid thigh
- Enter laterally aiming for intermuscular plane

## Anatomy
- Saphenous nerve sits lateral to femoral artery & vein
- Nerve to vastus medialis also important
- Sits within the vasto–interfascial plane between sartorius & vastus medialis

## Technique
- Enter lateral to your USS probe
- Use muscle twitches to stimulate vastus medialis & protect it
- Use this to place 10 mL at nerve to VM & then proceed to saphenous, and place another 10 mL
