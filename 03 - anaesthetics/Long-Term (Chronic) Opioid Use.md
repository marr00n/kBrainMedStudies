## Definition
- **By time:** greater than 3 months  
- **By dose:** >120 mg/day (morphine equivalent)

## Patient Groups
- Cancer pain  
- Chronic non-cancer pain  
- Addicts

## Key Terms

### Tolerance
- Decreased sensitivity to opioids  
- Require higher doses  
- Report less pain (↓ PONV)

### Dependence
- Physiological state characterised by withdrawal symptoms  
- Occurs when opioids are withdrawn or an antagonist is administered

### Addiction
- Pattern of behaviour to avoid withdrawal despite harm  
- Compulsive use

## Withdrawal
- Symptoms occur on cessation or antagonism of opioids

## Opioid-Induced Hyperalgesia
- Paradoxical situation where opioids lead to a pronociceptive state  
- Complex physiology  
- Treated by reducing opioid use  
- Can occur with short-term use of high opioid agonists or with chronic use
