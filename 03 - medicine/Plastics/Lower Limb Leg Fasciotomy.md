_Anterolateral incision_
- 15cm long incision, 2cm anterior to fibula
- overlies anterior intermuscular septum
- lateral comparment, protect peroneal nerve
- anterior compartment

  
![](http://127.0.0.1:61987/lateral%20incision.jpg)  

  
_Posteromedial incision_
- 15cm long incision, 2cm posterior to tibial head
- overlies posterior intermuscular septum
- deep flexor compartment, anterior. protect saphenous vein and nerve
- superficial flexor compartment just beneath, posterior. protect tibial NVB
