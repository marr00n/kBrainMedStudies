**Defn**: Death of the lunate bone

**Causes**
- Avascular necrosis of the lunate
- [Ulnar variance](Ulnar%20variance.md)
	- due to the shortened ulnar bone, the lunate doesn't get support and instead rides on the radius. This compresses it causing reduced vascularity and it dies.


Management
- STT fusion - stop the scaphoid from falling volarly
- Scaphocapitate fusion - stop the scaphoid from falling volarly
- Proximal row carpectomy - leaves reduced ROM
