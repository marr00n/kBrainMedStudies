[[melanoma]]

Measured from:
- top of the granular layer of the epidermis (or, if the surface is ulcerated, from the base of the ulcer)
To the:
- deepest invasive cell across the broad base of the tumour (dermal/subcutaneous)
If there is invasion deep along a periadnexal structure eg nerve. We do not measure this
