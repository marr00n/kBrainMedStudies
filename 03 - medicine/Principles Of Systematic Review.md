- EBM: best evidence, clinical experience of clinician, stakeholders input

- 47 types of Reviews. need to follow the required method for that review
	- Literature review - Quick dirty look
	- Narrative review - 'Emience-Based medicine'
		- often invited review article. not systematic, author bias
	- Systematic Review
		- systematic evaluation
		- specific question, pre-defined criteria to identify, select, assess and summarise.
		- Requires 1) thorough search, 2) appraisal of study quality, 3) assessment of heterogeneity
		- PICOST = PICO + Study Design +/- Time Period
