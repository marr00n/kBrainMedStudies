**Equipment**
- Cophyenylcaine & 1% lid w/ ad
- 11 blade
- Suction (use as tongue depressor)
- Head light
- Curved Artery
- Vomit bag and tissues for pt
- MCS swab

**Set up**
- Upright with support behind head
- Spray throat
- Stab incision
- MCS swab
- Open with mosquito
- Suction
- Wash is optional

**Landmark**
- Trace a line superiorly from the medial surface of the molars until it meets with a line traced horizontally from the base of the uvula. This is where the quinsy lies. Imagine a slightly curved line centred around on this landmark, parallel to the roof of the mouth (see photo below).
![](../../05%20-%20Hide/Media%20&%20Files/Pasted%20Image%2020230810141325.png)

**Admit**
- IVC + bloods (FBC, UEC, CRP, EBV monospot & serology)
- IV dex, IV hydration
- IV benzyl penicillin 1.2g QID & PO metro 500mg TDS
	- not amoxicillin, risk of EBV amoxicillin rash
	- PO clinda 500mg TDS
- Head elevation
- Salt gargles

**Discharge**
- 10/7 PO phenoxymethylpenicillin
- No routine follow up, unless concern for cancer, or >40 and smoker.
- Salt gargles at home
- Remain metro for half a week
