An objective measure of how easily the tympanic membrane vibrates and at what pressure it does so most easily (pressure admittance function).

If the middle ear is filled with fluid (eg, OME), vibration is impaired, and the result is a flat, or nearly flat, tracing

If the middle ear is filled with air but at a higher or lower pressure than the surrounding atmosphere, the peak on the graph will be shifted in position based on the pressure (to the left if negative, to the right if positive).
