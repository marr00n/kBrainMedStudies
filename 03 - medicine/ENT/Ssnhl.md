**History**
- *Unilateral*, sudden, hearing loss
- May have tinnitus and vertigo
- Shouldn't have otalgia, discharge or neuro signs
- no history of head trauma, recent ear surgery or any signs of systemic illness

**Exam**
[Rinne and weber hearing test](Rinne%20and%20weber%20hearing%20test.md)
Facial nerve Ax
Otoscopy:
- check for vesicles
- logical diagnostic criteria for SSNHL (the rule of three): 
	- sensorineural loss of worse than 30dB; 
	- in three consecutive frequencies;
	- within three days (72 hours).
