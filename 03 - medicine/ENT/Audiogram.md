
**Air conduction**
“X” is used for left ear AC
“O” is used for right ear AC
  
**Bone conduction**
“>” for the left ear BC
“<” or “\[” for the right ear BC

*Device is placed behind the ear in order to transmit sound through the vibration of the mastoid bone.*
