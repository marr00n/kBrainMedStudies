- 2 parts
	- <u>Pars Tensa</u>: taunt, thich and translucent
	- <u>Pars Flaccida</u>: thin and slack

- <u>Annulus</u>: fibrocartilaginous ring around TM that attaches to the temporal bone
- Malleus
	- <u>Umbo</u>: adherent part of TM to the handle of the Malleus, max point of convexity
	- <u>Manubrium</u>: visible through the TM. It leaves an impression called <u>malleolar stria</u>

**Layers**
- Outer circular layer: hairless keratinised squamous epithelium
- Intermediate fibrous layer
	- Outer radiating layer fibres
	- Inner circular layer of fibres
- Inner mucousal: single layer of ciliated columnar epithelium

**Innervation**
- Inner / medial surface: tympanic br of glossopharyngeal n
- Outer / lateral surface:
	- auriculotemporal br from mandibular (V3) - anterior half
	- auricular br from vagus (X) - posterior half
	- facial nerve
	- glossopharyngeal n

**Blood supply**
<u>Deep auricular</u> and <u>Anterior Tympanic arteries</u> from maxillary artery
<u>Stylomastoid artery</u> branch of the posterior auricular artery
<u>Inferior tympanic artery</u> branch of the ascending pharyngeal artery
