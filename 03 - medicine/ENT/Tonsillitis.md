**Watch out**
- [Epiglottitis](Epiglottitis.md) often presents with solely severe throat pain. Other Sx = severe dysphagia, hoarse/croaky voice, and fever .
- Trismus & 'hot potato' voice, and stertor (snoring sound of pharyngeal obstruction) point to a [Peritonsillar Abscess (Quinsy)](Peritonsillar%20Abscess%20(Quinsy).md)

**History**
- Odynophagia, Dysphagia, Fever, Malaise
- Anorexia
- Swollen tonsils with or without exudate
- 'Thick' or 'hot potato' voice (not hoarse voice)
- Lymphadenopathy (especially Level II/III)
- Referred otalgia (the glossopharyngeal nerve has a tympanic branch)

**Exam**
Posterior arch is obscured by tonsils
