
**Triad**
- Rapid onset aphagia or severe *<u>dysphagia<u></u></u>*, frequently associated with a severe sore throat
- Rapid onset laryngeal <u>voice change</u>: hoarse, croaky, husky or no voice
- Systemically very <u>unwell</u>: pyrexia, tachycardia, tachypnoea

**Differential**
Patient very unwell; rare: 
- Deep neck space infection eg:
- Retropharyngeal abscess
- Parapharyngeal abscess
Patient moderately unwell; common:
- Croup (in children)
- Quinsy or tonsillitis
Patient slightly unwell; common: 
- Laryngitis

**Immediate management**
- Broad spectrum ABs
- IV dex 8mg TDS
- adrenaline nebs prn
- NBM
- humidified O2
- IVH
