**Request form 1 (to be done first):**

Fasting plasma metanephrines, aldosterone, renin, U&Es, calcium, glucose, TFTs etc.  Late night salivary cortisol should be done before dexamethasone is taken.

 
**Request form 2:**

1 mg DST or 1 mg dex suppression (best test for adrenal incidentaloma if patient is not on oestrogen therapy, anticonvulsants)
