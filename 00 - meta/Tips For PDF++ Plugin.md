**Rectangular selection embed (best for visual reference)**

Embed a specified rectangular area from a PDF directly into your note. [Obsidian PDF++](https://ryotaushio.github.io/obsidian-pdf-plus/embedding-rectangular-selections.html):

1. Open the PDF in PDF++ and navigate to the table
2. Hold `Alt` and drag over the table to make a rectangular selection
3. A toolbar will appear — click "Copy embed link to rectangular selection"
4. Paste into your note — it renders as a cropped image of that exact table region
